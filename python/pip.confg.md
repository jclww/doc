### 文件目录
#### 全局
`/etc/pip.conf`  
`/Library/Application Support/pip/pip.conf`



#### 用户目录
`$HOME/.config/pip/pip.conf`  
`$HOME/.pip/pip.conf`  
`$HOME/Library/Application Support/pip/pip.conf`  

#### 项目VM
`$VIRTUAL_ENV/pip.conf`
